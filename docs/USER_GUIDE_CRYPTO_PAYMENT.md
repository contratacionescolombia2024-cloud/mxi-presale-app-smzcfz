
# User Guide: Purchasing MXI with Crypto

This guide explains how to purchase MXI tokens using cryptocurrency (USDT BEP20).

## What You Need

Before you start, make sure you have:

1. **A Web Browser**
   - Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge
   - Crypto payments are only available on web (not mobile app)

2. **A Crypto Wallet**
   - MetaMask (recommended)
   - Trust Wallet
   - Or any WalletConnect-compatible wallet

3. **USDT BEP20 Tokens**
   - Minimum: 20 USDT
   - Maximum: 50,000 USDT
   - Must be on Binance Smart Chain (BSC)

4. **BNB for Gas Fees**
   - Small amount needed for transaction fees
   - Usually 0.001-0.01 BNB per transaction

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1: Open the App in Web Browser

1. Go to the MXI Presale website
2. Log in to your account
3. You should see the home screen

### Step 2: Connect Your Wallet

1. Click the **"Crypto Payment"** button on the home screen
2. You'll see the "Connect Wallet" screen
3. Click the **Web3Modal button** (it will show wallet options)
4. Choose your wallet:
   - **MetaMask** - If you have the browser extension
   - **WalletConnect** - For mobile wallets or other options
   - **Trust Wallet** - If you have Trust Wallet

5. Approve the connection in your wallet
6. Your wallet address and USDT balance will appear

### Step 3: Purchase MXI

1. After connecting, click **"Purchase MXI with USDT"**
2. You'll see the purchase screen with:
   - Current MXI price
   - Your USDT balance
   - Purchase form

3. Enter the amount of USDT you want to spend
   - Minimum: 20 USDT
   - Maximum: 50,000 USDT

4. The app will automatically calculate how much MXI you'll receive

5. Review the details:
   - USDT amount you're paying
   - MXI amount you'll receive
   - Current price per MXI

6. Click **"Pay [amount] USDT"**

### Step 4: Confirm Transaction

1. Your wallet will open with a transaction confirmation
2. Review the transaction details:
   - Recipient address
   - USDT amount
   - Gas fee (in BNB)

3. Click **"Confirm"** in your wallet
4. Wait for the transaction to be sent

### Step 5: Wait for Confirmation

1. You'll see a confirmation screen with:
   - Transaction hash
   - Current status
   - Transaction details

2. The transaction needs **3 blockchain confirmations**
   - This usually takes 1-2 minutes
   - You can close the screen and check back later

3. You can click **"View on BscScan"** to see the transaction on the blockchain

### Step 6: Receive Your MXI

1. After 3 confirmations, your MXI tokens will be automatically credited
2. You'll see:
   - "Payment Confirmed" status
   - Updated MXI balance
   - Transaction details

3. Your MXI tokens are now in your account!
4. They will start earning vesting rewards immediately

## Troubleshooting

### "Wallet not connected"
**Solution:** Click "Connect Wallet" first before trying to purchase.

### "Insufficient USDT balance"
**Solution:** Make sure you have enough USDT BEP20 tokens in your wallet.

### "Insufficient BNB for gas fees"
**Solution:** Add some BNB to your wallet. You need a small amount for transaction fees.

### "Please switch to BSC network"
**Solution:** 
1. Open your wallet
2. Click on the network selector
3. Choose "Binance Smart Chain" or "BSC"
4. Try again

### "Transaction rejected by user"
**Solution:** You cancelled the transaction in your wallet. Try again and click "Confirm".

### Transaction is taking too long
**Solution:**
1. Check the transaction on BscScan (click "View on BscScan")
2. If it's pending, wait a bit longer
3. If it failed, try again with a higher gas fee
4. Contact support if it's been more than 10 minutes

### MXI not credited after confirmation
**Solution:**
1. Wait a few more minutes
2. Refresh the page
3. Check your balance in the home screen
4. If still not credited after 10 minutes, contact support with your transaction hash

## FAQ

### Q: Can I use the mobile app?
**A:** No, crypto payments are only available on the web version. Open the website in your mobile browser.

### Q: Which wallets are supported?
**A:** MetaMask, Trust Wallet, and any WalletConnect-compatible wallet.

### Q: Can I pay with other cryptocurrencies?
**A:** Currently, only USDT BEP20 is supported. More options may be added in the future.

### Q: What's the minimum purchase amount?
**A:** 20 USDT

### Q: What's the maximum purchase amount?
**A:** 50,000 USDT per transaction

### Q: How long does it take?
**A:** Usually 1-2 minutes for 3 blockchain confirmations.

### Q: Can I cancel a transaction?
**A:** Once confirmed in your wallet and sent to the blockchain, transactions cannot be cancelled.

### Q: Is it safe?
**A:** Yes! All transactions are verified on the blockchain and require 3 confirmations.

### Q: What if I send to the wrong address?
**A:** The app automatically sends to the correct address. You cannot change it.

### Q: Can I get a refund?
**A:** Blockchain transactions are irreversible. Make sure you enter the correct amount before confirming.
